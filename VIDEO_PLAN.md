# ShopSage — Product Video Plan

**Style:** Clean, modern app ad. Think Notion, Linear, Arc Browser. Let the product do the talking.
**Length:** ~2 minutes
**Production:** Screen recordings of the app (phone mockup) + royalty-free music + voiceover. No camera needed.
**Tone:** Calm confidence. Not hype. "This just makes your life easier."

---

## Tools Needed

- Screen recorder (OBS, Loom, or built-in macOS/Windows)
- Video editor (CapCut, DaVinci Resolve free, iMovie — anything)
- Royalty-free music: [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/music/) or [Uppbeat](https://uppbeat.io/) — search "lo-fi focus" or "minimal tech"
- Optional: [ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io) free tier for a polished AI voiceover, or record yourself

## Editing Notes

- **Pacing:** The hook is slow and deliberate. Once the app appears, the pace picks up — but never feels rushed.
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter or SF Pro) for text overlays. White on black or near-black on white only. No drop shadows.
- **Music arc:** Near-silent on hook → gentle presence on reveal → builds slowly → peaks on montage → fades on outro.
- **Color grading:** None needed. The app's clean white UI is the visual identity.
- **Captions:** Add subtitles for every VO line. This will be watched on mute a significant portion of the time.
- **Aspect ratio:** Export in 9:16 (vertical) for social/mobile, and 16:9 for YouTube/desktop. The phone mockup works in both.

## Recording Tips (zero-budget)

- Use the `?autoplay=true` URL param — the hands-free demo handles all the interactions for you. Just hit record.
- Record at 2x resolution if possible, export at 1080p — looks professional.
- Shoot multiple takes of each segment and pick the cleanest one.
- If your mic adds room noise, ElevenLabs free tier (voices: "Adam", "Sarah", or "Daniel") produces clean results — just paste the VO script above.
- For the text-only hook, any video editor can do a simple fade-in on a black background. No special effects needed.
